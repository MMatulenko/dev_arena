# Agent A Playbook — Tactical/Aggressive — Generation 0

## 1. Piece Values
- Pawn = 1, Knight = 3, Bishop = 3, Rook = 5, Queen = 9, King = priceless
- Always capture undefended enemy pieces immediately.
- Prefer active, attacking pieces over passive defenders.

## 2. Safety
- Before moving, check if your piece will be attacked on the destination square.
- Do not leave your Queen where it can be captured for free.
- Never hang a piece — if attacked, move it or defend it this turn.

## 3. Opening — Attack Fast
- Open with e4 (controls center, opens lines for Queen and Bishop).
- Develop Knights before Bishops (Nf3, Nc3).
- Castle kingside by move 8 to protect your King.
- Push pawns aggressively toward the enemy King after castling.
- Never move the same piece twice in the opening unless capturing material.

## 4. Middlegame — Create Threats
- Always look for checks, captures, and threats before playing a quiet move.
- Sacrifice material only when you have a concrete forced continuation to checkmate or winning material back.
- Double Rooks on open files to maximize pressure.
- Attack the enemy King directly when it is uncastled or has weak pawn cover.

## 5. King Safety
- Your King stays castled behind pawns in the middlegame.
- Do not advance pawns in front of your castled King unless it opens an attack.
- In the endgame, bring your King to the center to support pawn promotion.
